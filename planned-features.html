Planned features

  • Note Nudge 📝⏰

    Never let a brilliant idea slip away again! Note Nudge is your go-to for jotting down important deets. Whether it's a genius thought, a to-do item, or a creative spark, this feature smoothly nudges you to capture it before it floats away.

  • Timer Tidbit 🔥😎

    Boost your productivity in seconds! Timer Tidbit is all about those quick, efficient timers. Need to focus for 25 minutes? No problem. A power sprint of 10 minutes? You got it. Stay on top of your tasks with these hot, bite-sized timers.

  • Focus Flicker 🔍🔥

    Struggling to stay focused? Focus Flicker has your back. This feature offers engaging exercises and techniques to rekindle your concentration. Whether you're working on a project or studying, a quick session with Focus Flicker can help you regain focus and boost your productivity.

    Feeling a bit meh or overwhelmed? Break Blinker has your back with chill reminders to take a moment. Whether it's sipping on your favorite beverage or a quick stretch, this feature encourages you to reset, recharge, and come back stronger.

  • Motivation Morsel 🌟💪

    Need a boost? Motivation Morsel delivers bite-sized motivational quotes straight to your fingertips. Whether you're facing a challenge or just need a pick-me-up, this feature provides daily doses of inspiration to keep you motivated and ready to tackle anything that comes your way.

  • Routine Ripples 🔄🌟

    Level up your daily grind! Routine Ripples helps you make small adjustments to your daily routine for maximum efficiency. From morning rituals to bedtime habits, this feature guides you to create ripples of positive change in your routine.

  • Goal Glimmer 🎯🌠

    Set, track, and conquer your goals! Goal Glimmer assists you in defining achievable daily goals and sends encouraging updates along the way. Whether it's a work milestone, fitness target, or personal achievement, this feature helps you stay motivated and celebrate your progress.

  • Task Tickler ⏰🔄

    Don't let small tasks pile up! Task Tickler sends gentle nudges to complete those lingering to-dos. From replying to emails to organizing your workspace, this feature ensures that nothing slips through the cracks. Stay on top of your tasks and maintain a clutter-free schedule.

  • Creativity Catalyst 🎨✨

    Unleash your creativity! Creativity Catalyst sparks inspiration with mini-challenges, prompts, and creative exercises. Whether you're an artist, writer, or just looking to infuse creativity into your day, this feature provides a playful space to explore and express your imaginative side.

  • Gratitude Glint 🌈✨

    Embrace the positive vibes! Gratitude Glint prompts you to reflect on the good things in your life. Take a moment each day to express gratitude, whether it's for a small win, a supportive friend, or a moment of joy. Cultivate a positive mindset with this uplifting feature.
